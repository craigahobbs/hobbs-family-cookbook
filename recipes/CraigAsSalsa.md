~~~ recipe-info
Title: Craig A.'s Salsa
~~~

Using a food processor, process until chunky:

~~~ recipe-ingredients
3 cloves of garlic

3 serrano peppers

1 habanero pepper (optional)

1/2 white onion

6 ripe roma tomatoes

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp dried cilantro

1/2 tsp lemon juice
~~~

Serve with crisp tortilla chips.
